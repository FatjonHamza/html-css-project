Our services at a glance

The March Group offers you a wide range of construction services. From road construction and horticulture to demolition and civil engineering, we cover all aspects of your project with expertise and attention to detail. Whether road construction, garden design, demolition or infrastructure development - we implement your visions with the highest precision and excellence.

Road construction

As specialists in road construction, we ensure efficient and well thought-out transport networks. From motorways to city streets - our expertise ensures you a durable and safe infrastructure.

Horticulture

Our goal in horticulture is to create impressive outdoor spaces. Whether you want an artistically landscaped garden, a peaceful patio or a thriving green oasis, our team will turn your dreams into reality.

Demolition

Demolition work is an essential part of the construction process. Our demolition services are carried out with the utmost precision, always with strict adherence to safety standards. This is how we pave the way for future developments.

Civil Engineering

Infrastructural development is at the center of our work, with civil engineering playing a key role. Whether foundations, drainage systems, supply lines or underground structures - our expertise in civil engineering creates the solid foundation that is essential for successful construction projects.

Immerse yourself in our diverse portfolio of impressive construction projects. Revitalization of the city boulevard

A once busy city street has been transformed into a bustling and pedestrian-friendly destination.With the integration of innovative urban design elements, state-of-the-art lighting and carefully selected landscaping, we have created an inviting space for city dwellers and visitors alike.

Serenity Garden Retreat

Experience the peace and serenity at our 'Serenity Garden Retreat'. In this project we managed to create a peaceful oasis in a limited space. With careful design, harmonious selection of plants and the soothing sound of trickling water features, we have created a secluded retreat that offers a sanctuary from everyday stress.

Backyard remodel

Through detailed planning and professional implementation, we managed to breathe new life into an inconspicuous backyard. With the introduction of new landscaping elements, the creation of an elegant terrace and the integration of functional elements such as a fire pit and cozy seating area, the once-unassuming space now shines with new splendor.

Demolition of an old house

With meticulous precision and strict compliance with safety regulations, we dismantled the structure to ensure efficient debris removal and material reuse. By effectively coordinating the entire demolition process, we gave our clients a fresh start.
